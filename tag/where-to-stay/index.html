Where to Stay

Hotel Rosario

This Hotel is located at the centre of the town,  just off the main square. It has a breakfast room with garden, bar, TV room and internet point. It is just a few steps away from the Parish Church.

Address:

Pietrelcina (BN)

Lombardi Park Hotel

This is the Largest hotel in the area offering ample accommodation for individuals, families and groups. A one minute walk from the Convent Church and the Padre Pio Museum and a 5 minute walk from the old town.

Il Castello Dei Templari

This is a family run hotel near Pietrelcina. It is located in the countryside about three km from the town.

Good place to stay if you want to be out in the countryside but close enough to the town to be able to get there by car in 2 minutes. Can be booked by individuals, families or medium sized groups. It can accommodate 22 people at one time and has a large car park.  Wi-fi is also available.

Guest House Padre Pio


Guest House “Padre Pio”, is family run and offers accommodation in Pietrelcina town, a few meters from the Convent of the Capuchin Friars and the Museum of Padre Pio. It is also just a five minute walk away from the birthplace of Padre Pio.

There is parking close by and a shuttle service to Piana Romana is possible. It is ‘ideal for those who want to experience a sense of peace and spirituality in the birthplace of St. Pio of Pietrelcina.

Facilities include a room with bathroom, air conditioning, tv room and other amenities.
