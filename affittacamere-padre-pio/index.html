Guest House Padre Pio

Print 🖨 PDF 📄 eBook 📱


Guest House “Padre Pio”, is family run and offers accommodation in Pietrelcina town, a few meters from the Convent of the Capuchin Friars and the Museum of Padre Pio. It is also just a five minute walk away from the birthplace of Padre Pio.

There is parking close by and a shuttle service to Piana Romana is possible. It is ‘ideal for those who want to experience a sense of peace and spirituality in the birthplace of St. Pio of Pietrelcina.

Facilities include a room with bathroom, air conditioning, tv room and other amenities.
